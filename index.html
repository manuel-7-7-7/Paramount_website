Who We Serve

Property types we service

Every property is different — our crews adapt the scope and schedule to fit.

Daycares

Daycares demand a higher standard of clean. We disinfect high-touch surfaces and play areas with safe, kid-appropriate products, keeping little ones healthy and parents at ease.

Condos

From lobbies to hallways to amenity spaces, we help condo boards and property managers maintain a polished first impression for residents and guests — with a particular focus on low-rise and mid-rise buildings.

Office Spaces

A clean workspace supports a productive team. We keep desks, common areas, boardrooms, and washrooms sanitized and presentable, day or night, without disrupting the workday.

Barbershops

A clean shop builds trust. We handle the daily detail work — floors, stations, and washrooms — so your chairs stay full and clients keep coming back.

Dealerships

Showroom floors should shine as bright as the cars on them. We keep glass, floors, and display areas spotless so every vehicle gets the showcase it deserves.

Supermarkets

High foot traffic means high demands. We keep aisles, entryways, and washrooms clean and sanitized, so shoppers feel confident every time they walk through your doors.

Warehouses

Big spaces need a thorough approach. We manage floor care, dust control, and waste removal across your facility, keeping operations safe, compliant, and efficient.

Retail

First impressions drive sales. We keep your floors, fitting rooms, and storefronts guest-ready, so shoppers can focus on browsing — not clutter.
